Our Mission

The Security Operations Center (SOC) is the front line of defense in today’s digital battlefield. Our mission is to safeguard critical systems, investigate threats, and train the next generation of cybersecurity defenders.

By joining our program, you will gain hands-on experience with real tools, real incidents, and real teamwork — preparing you for the challenges of a Tier 1 SOC Analyst role.

The SOC Training Path

From the basics of Tier 1 responsibilities to advanced defense strategies, here’s how you’ll be forged into a cyber defender:

  • Step 1: Networking Fund.
    • TCP/IP Model
